## The brand

BeyondTikTok is our dedicated TikTok practice, an Official TikTok Marketing Partner covering China, Southeast Asia, France, the UK and the USA. The offer pulls together TikTok video production, creator partnerships, paid ads, livestream commerce, and TikTok Shop operations under one roof.

## The challenge

The new offer needed its own home on the web. The brief was clear: a site that generates qualified leads for TikTok service requests, renders cleanly on desktop and mobile, and ranks well enough in search to bring in organic traffic without constant paid spend.

## Our approach

We treated the site as a lead-generation product, not a brochure. The information architecture was built around the questions prospects actually ask when they evaluate a TikTok partner, with clear service breakdowns, regional coverage, and proof points surfaced fast. Visual design carried the BeyondTikTok brand system across every screen size.

On the performance side, we shipped the site with structured SEO foundations from day one, covering metadata, semantic markup, page speed, and a keyword map aligned to service intent. We layered Google Ads campaigns targeted at high-intent queries so paid and organic reinforced each other rather than competing.

## Results

The site launched as a working funnel for the BeyondTikTok offer: SEO-optimized for organic reach, UX-optimized for conversion, and paired with tailored Google Ads campaigns to extend visibility across priority markets.
