## The brand

ROC Skincare is a renowned French skincare brand, celebrated for scientifically proven and dermatologist-tested products that target multiple skin concerns with key ingredients such as retinol, vitamin C, and hyaluronic acid. As a leader in anti-aging solutions, ROC is committed to high-quality, cruelty-free skincare innovation.

## The challenge

As a branding agency, we were tasked with localizing ROC Skincare for the Chinese market. The mandate was broad. It covered adapting the brand story, experience, visual identity, logo, and naming, as well as establishing photography, video, and web design guidelines. ROC needed a presence in China that felt genuinely French without feeling foreign, and genuinely local without drifting from the global brand.

## Our approach

Our approach leaned on a deep understanding of both French elegance and Chinese cultural nuances, so the brand's core DNA stayed intact while becoming relevant and appealing to the Chinese consumer. We rebuilt each brand layer in sequence: positioning and story first, then naming, then the visual identity system, then packaging and application guidelines. Every decision was tested against a simple question. Would a Chinese consumer understand this as premium, effective, and clinically credible?

## Results

The result is a meticulously localized ROC Skincare presence in China that resonates with local preferences without compromising the brand's foundational values and identity. By blending ROC's French heritage with tailored elements for the Chinese market, we crafted a brand story and visual identity that speak to the sophisticated, health-conscious Chinese consumer. The localization extends across all brand touchpoints, producing a cohesive, immersive brand experience that holds to ROC's global standards of excellence.
