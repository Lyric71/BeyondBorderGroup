## The brand

Exeed is the premium automotive brand from China's Chery Group, expanding into Eastern Europe, the Middle East, and South America. Competing against established players like Mercedes-Benz and BMW, Exeed positions itself on quality and design, with its luxury electric line EXLANTIX counting over 180,000 global users and a roadmap to reach more than 25 countries by 2025.

## The challenge

The task was to lift Exeed's global presence and reshape public perception in markets where Chinese premium automotive is still a young category. That meant doing credible PR at scale across very different media environments, while building a brand story strong enough to hold its own against legacy European names.

## Our approach

Since August 2023, we have run a continuous PR program combining earned media, content, and executive visibility. The work spans original editorial, advertorials, media interview planning, Q&A sessions, crisis management, CEO speeches, and presentations tailored for major industry stages.

We layered branding and digital innovation thinking on top of classic PR execution so the message stayed consistent from a trade show booth to a social post to a CEO keynote. Flagship moments included the Geneva Motor Show, the Global Co-creation Ecological Conference, and the EXLANTIX pre-launch campaigns, each built as a reinforcing chapter in the same brand narrative rather than one-off activations.

## Results

The program generated over 2.5 billion impressions for the Global Co-creation Ecological Conference, 60 million+ impressions at the Geneva Motor Show, 50 million+ views for the EXLANTIX launch campaign, and 2 million+ views for the RX model release, establishing Exeed as a credible luxury player in its target overseas markets.
