## The brand

Blue is Hong Kong's first digital life insurer. The proposition is built around removing the friction of traditional life insurance: no long forms, no jargon, no paperwork. Customers answer a short set of online questions and can get covered in minutes, directly on mobile or desktop.

## The challenge

Launching the first digital life insurer in a market dominated by agent-led incumbents is a trust problem as much as a product problem. Blue needed a digital presence that felt credible enough for life insurance, simple enough for a quick sign-up, and visible enough to build an audience from a standing start, in both Chinese and English.

## Our approach

We treated the website and the launch campaign as a single product.

- Designed a bilingual Chinese and English site optimized for mobile and desktop
- Built an intuitive interface around the short-form quote and sign-up flow
- Developed SEO-oriented content to compete for high-intent insurance queries
- Ran social media and digital advertising to build the audience and feed the funnel
- Shaped the campaign around lead generation, with every creative pointing to a clear next step

The content strategy kept technical language out of the front door while still satisfying the trust signals a life insurance buyer needs to see.

## Results

Within the first month, Blue gained 10,000 new social media followers. In the first week alone, the site generated more than 3,000 newsletter sign-ups, giving the brand a real owned audience from day one and a working digital pipeline for ongoing acquisition in Hong Kong.
