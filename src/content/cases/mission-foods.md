## The brand

Mission Foods prides itself on delivering authentic Mexican products, driven by the pursuit of fresh ingredients, simple recipes, diverse flavors, and wholesome products. In China, the brand extends its expertise beyond tortillas with a selection of corn chips crafted to capture the essence of Mexican cuisine, balancing flavor and crunch for everything from casual snacking to entertaining guests.

## The challenge

Our assignment involved leading social media campaigns and maintaining continuous content for Mission Foods on WeChat, Weibo and RED, all in support of introducing the corn chips range to the Chinese marketplace.

## Our approach

To engage Gen Z in China with the corn chips launch, we centered the strategy on snacking moments that resonate with local food preferences and tastes. Content highlighted the uniqueness and versatility of corn chips as a snack, appealing to Gen Z's appetite for innovative and flavorful food experiences. That included integrating corn chips into traditional Chinese snacks and meals in a way that felt both authentic and respectful of local culinary traditions. On WeChat, Weibo and RED, we worked with influencers and built interactive campaigns that encouraged sharing and participation, keeping messaging aligned with Gen Z values and lifestyle.

## Results

The strategy delivered notable success, particularly with the Halloween campaign, driving follower growth and higher engagement across WeChat, Weibo and RED. A consistent mix of news, recipes and deals kept the brand visible and growing, marking a strong presence in the Chinese snacking market.
