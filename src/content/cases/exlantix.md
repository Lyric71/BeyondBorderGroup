## The brand

Exlantix is a new premium automotive line within the Exceed portfolio of the Chery Automotive group. It is distinguished by its integration of advanced technology and luxury, set out to redefine the essence of driving with a global perspective.

## The challenge

We were tasked with creating a distinctive visual identity for Exlantix that broke away from traditional Chinese design influences and embodied a universally appealing design philosophy. The goal was to capture the brand's luxurious nature and its ambitions on a global stage, ensuring resonance across markets.

## Our approach

The work spanned the full identity system, from emblem to typography, color schemes and imagery. Each element was developed to echo Exlantix's dedication to quality and its pioneering spirit in the luxury automotive segment. Applied consistently across touchpoints, the identity supports a coherent and captivating brand presence wherever the brand appears.

## Results

The outcome is a comprehensive visual identity that captures Exlantix's innovative ethos and premium stature. It flows across all brand interactions to create a unified experience and lays a robust foundation for the brand's international unveiling.
